Frequently Asked Questions

How does the experience work?

After booking, you'll receive a digital guide with your starting location and instructions. Use your phone to access clues, solve puzzles, and navigate between locations at your own pace.

Can we play as a group?

Absolutely! Our mysteries are designed for 3-10 people. Everyone in your group will need to purchase a ticket.

How long does each story take?

Stories range from 2-5 hours depending on difficulty and your pace. You can pause and resume at any time.

What if the weather is bad?

You can reschedule your adventure for any date. Many locations have indoor waiting areas if you need a break.
